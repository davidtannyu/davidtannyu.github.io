David Tan

Hi there! I am a software developer who programs with Ruby on Rails, React, and Javascript.
I have had a fondness for problem solving ever since I was first introduced to math. Even as a Computer and Electrical Engineer major back in college, I took courses in Computer Science and Math such as Data Structures and Discrete Math. After my work at Epic which introduced more programming experience with a new language, I decided to take the chance to fine-tune my skills, thus attending App Academy.
At App Academy, my skills were put to the test whether it was learning quickly, pair programming, communication, and coding. In the end, I had the pleasure of building my own full stack project called QueueOverfilled.
Now, I'm looking for oppurtunities where I can not only hone these skills, but also learn new ones to complement my skill set and broaden my horizons.
